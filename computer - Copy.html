Computer Science

Computer is the study of computer and computational systems .Computer scieence is one of the courses offered in kwara state university under the college of information communication technology(I.C.T) and there different courses offerd in this department from hundred level(100L) to four-hundred level(400L).


The class is split into three(3) categories,Which are;

  1. REGULAR STUDENT
  2. PROFESSIONAL DEGREE PROGRAM(PDP) STUDENT
  3. IDEL STUDENT

REGULAR STUDENT

These are student accepted for enrollment into the institution for the purpose of obtaining Bachelor degree(B.Sc) and NYSC Certificate after attending the program.
