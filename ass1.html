The class is split into three(3) categories,Which are;

  • REGULAR STUDENT
  • PROFESSIONAL DEGREE PROGRAM(PDP) STUDENT
  • IDEL STUDENT

    • REGULAR STUDENT

    PROFESSIONAL DEGREE PROGRAM(PDP) STUDENT

    IDEL STUDENT


    These are student acceptedfor enroollment into the institution for the purpose of obtaining Bachelor's degree(B.Sc) only.
